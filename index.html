About Us

Wells Government Equipment and Supply LLC is a veteran-owned small business specializing in the sourcing, packaging, and delivery of industrial hardware and mission-critical supplies to federal agencies. With over 36 years of federal procurement experience, we bring deep knowledge of acquisition workflows, compliance standards, and supply chain coordination. Our focus is on reliability, responsiveness, and operational precision—making us a trusted partner for GPC purchases, Army logistics, and subcontracting support.

Capability Statement

Wells Government Equipment and Supply LLC is a veteran-owned small business specializing in the sourcing, packaging, and delivery of industrial hardware and mission-critical supplies to federal agencies. We support GPC micro-purchases, Army logistics, and pass-through subcontracting with a focus on compliance, responsiveness, and operational precision.

Our offerings include bolts, nuts, washers, springs, gaskets, control knobs, and packing materials, organized under PSCs 5310, 5306, 5305, 5360, 5330, 5355, and 5340. We deliver custom-labeled kits and crated shipments tailored to mission-specific requirements, and we operate under NAICS codes 423840, 488991, 561910, and 541611.

With over three decades of federal procurement experience, our team brings deep knowledge of acquisition workflows, documentation standards, and supply chain coordination. We are equipped to support federal buyers and prime contractors with scalable, compliant solutions across multiple acquisition pathways.
