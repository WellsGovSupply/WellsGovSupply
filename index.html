Capability Statement

Wells Government Equipment and Supply LLC is a certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) and Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) specializing in the sourcing, packaging, and delivery of industrial hardware and mission-critical supplies to federal agencies. We support GPC micro-purchases, Army logistics, and pass-through subcontracting with a focus on compliance, responsiveness, and operational precision.

Our offerings include bolts, nuts, washers, springs, gaskets, control knobs, and packing materials, organized under PSCs 5305, 5306, 5310, 5330, 5340, 5355, and 5360. We deliver custom-labeled kits and crated shipments tailored to mission-specific requirements, and we operate under NAICS codes 423710, 423830, 423840, 488991, 541611, and 561910.

With over three decades of federal procurement experience, our team brings deep knowledge of acquisition workflows, documentation standards, and supply chain coordination. We deliver scalable, compliant solutions to federal buyers and prime contractors across multiple acquisition pathways.
